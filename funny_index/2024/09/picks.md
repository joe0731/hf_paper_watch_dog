## 3. [Emu3: Next-Token Prediction is All You Need](https://huggingface.co/papers/2409.18869)

**`[NERF ALERT]`** | **ArXiv**: [2409.18869](https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.18869) | **Org**: BAAI | **Upvotes**: 97 | **Date**: 2024-09-30

**Skill: "One Ring" / 技能名：「一统天下」**

**Category**: `Multimodal` | **Tags**: `next-token-prediction`, `unified-modality`, `text-image-video`, `diffusion-alternative`

**Source**: [papers/2024/09/2024-09-30.md, Line 9](papers/2024/09/2024-09-30.md#L9)

**EN**: NERF ALERT: One Ring. Emu3 uses pure next-token prediction for everything — text, images, video. No diffusion, no separate image encoder. Tokenize all modalities, predict next token. It generates images rivaling diffusion models and videos rivaling specialized generators. One prediction mechanism to rule them all. The "multi-branch modality" meta just got unified into a single overpowered build.

**CN**: NERF 警告：一统天下。Emu3 用纯 next-token prediction 搞定一切——文本、图像、视频。没有 diffusion，没有独立图像编码器。全部模态 tokenize，预测下一个 token。生成的图像可与 diffusion 一战，视频可与专用生成器一战。一招鲜吃遍天。「多分支模态」meta 刚刚被统一成一个超模 build。
