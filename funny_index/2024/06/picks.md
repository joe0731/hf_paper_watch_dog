**Source**: [papers/2024/06/2024-06-13.md, Line 10](papers/2024/06/2024-06-13.md#L10)

**EN**: MUTATION: Dream Data. Feed an aligned LLM literally nothing — just the system prompt template — and it spontaneously generates instruction-response pairs. A passive skill that was always there, now activated.
