## 2. [Self-Rewarding Language Models](https://huggingface.co/papers/2401.10020)

**`[MUTATION]`** | **ArXiv**: [2401.10020](https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.10020) | **Org**: Meta AI | **Upvotes**: 152 | **Date**: 2024-01-18

**Skill: "Self-Grader" / 技能名：「自批自改」**

**Category**: `Training` | **Tags**: `self-reward, iterative-DPO, no-separate-RM, self-improvement`

**Source**: [papers/2024/01/2024-01-18.md, Line 9](papers/2024/01/2024-01-18.md#L9)

**EN**: MUTATION: Self-Grader. Instead of using a separate reward model, the LLM judges its OWN outputs and uses those judgments to train itself. It improves at both the task AND at judging, creating a self-improving loop. The model is grading its own homework and getting smarter from it. The training meta just evolved — no more external reward model required.

**CN**: 变异：自批自改。不再用独立的奖励模型，LLM 自己评判自己的输出，并用这些评判来训练自己。它在任务和评判两方面都变强，形成自我改进循环。模型在批改自己的作业，并从中变聪明。训练流派刚刚进化——不再需要外部奖励模型。

## 4. [Lumiere: A Space-Time Diffusion Model for Video Generation](https://huggingface.co/papers/2401.12945)

**`[NEW SKILL]`** | **ArXiv**: [2401.12945](https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.12945) | **Org**: Google Research | **Upvotes**: 87 | **Date**: 2024-01-24

**Skill: "One Take" / 技能名：「一镜到底」**

**Category**: `Vision` | **Tags**: `video-generation, space-time-diffusion, single-pass, temporal-coherence`

**Source**: [papers/2024/01/2024-01-24.md, Line 9](papers/2024/01/2024-01-24.md#L9)

**EN**: NEW SKILL UNLOCKED: One Take. Lumiere generates the ENTIRE temporal duration of a video in one pass using a Space-Time U-Net. No more generating keyframes then interpolating. A single forward pass produces temporally coherent, realistic motion. Video generation just got its "generate in one shot" moment. The "video creation" skill tree just learned a new combo.

**CN**: 新技能解锁：一镜到底。Lumiere 用 Space-Time U-Net 一次前向传播生成整段视频的完整时间维度。不再先生成关键帧再插值。单次前向传播即可产生时间连贯、逼真的运动。视频生成刚刚迎来了"一镜到底"时刻。"视频创作"技能树刚刚解锁新组合技。
