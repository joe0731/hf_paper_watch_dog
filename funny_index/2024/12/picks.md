**Source**: [papers/2024/12/2024-12-19.md, Line 10](papers/2024/12/2024-12-19.md#L10)

**EN**: NERF ALERT: BERT Reforged. Everyone's building decoder-only models. The result: a BERT-class model that's dramatically better for retrieval and classification. Sometimes the best move is revisiting an old friend with new tech. The classic encoder just got a full rework patch.
