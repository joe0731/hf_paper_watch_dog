## 1. [The Era of 1-bit LLMs: All Large Language Models are in 1.58 Bits](https://huggingface.co/papers/2402.17764)

**`[BOSS DROP]`** | **ArXiv**: [2402.17764](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.17764) | **Org**: Microsoft Research | **Upvotes**: 627 | **Date**: 2024-02-28

**Skill: "Ternary Ascension" / 技能名：「三值飞升」**

**Category**: `Efficiency` | **Tags**: `ternary-weights, 1-bit-LLM, quantization, BitNet`

**Source**: [papers/2024/02/2024-02-28.md, Line 9](papers/2024/02/2024-02-28.md#L9)

**EN**: BOSS DROP: Ternary Ascension. BitNet b1.58 compresses every parameter to one of three values: -1, 0, or 1. Matrix multiplications become simple additions. Yet it matches full-precision Transformer performance at the same model size, while being dramatically faster and more energy-efficient. The most upvoted paper of the year. The efficiency dungeon just dropped its legendary weapon.

**CN**: BOSS 掉落：三值飞升。BitNet b1.58 把每个参数压缩成三个值之一：-1、0 或 1。矩阵乘法变成简单加法。但在相同模型规模下仍能匹配全精度 Transformer 的性能，同时速度更快、能耗更低。年度最高赞论文。效率副本刚刚掉落了传说级武器。

## 3. [Self-Discover: Large Language Models Self-Compose Reasoning Structures](https://huggingface.co/papers/2402.03620)

**`[NEW SKILL]`** | **ArXiv**: [2402.03620](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.03620) | **Org**: Google DeepMind | **Upvotes**: 117 | **Date**: 2024-02-07

**Skill: "Build-a-Brain" / 技能名：「自组思维」**

**Category**: `Reasoning` | **Tags**: `reasoning-structure, chain-of-thought, modular-thinking, self-composition`

**Source**: [papers/2024/02/2024-02-07.md, Line 9](papers/2024/02/2024-02-07.md#L9)

**EN**: NEW SKILL UNLOCKED: Build-a-Brain. Instead of using a fixed chain-of-thought template, Self-Discover lets the model SELECT and COMPOSE its own reasoning structure from a set of atomic reasoning modules. The model builds its own thinking scaffolding before solving. It's like choosing which tools to bring before starting a quest. The reasoning skill tree just got a meta-branch.

**CN**: 新技能解锁：自组思维。不再使用固定的思维链模板，Self-Discover 让模型从一组原子推理模块中自行选择和组合推理结构。模型在解题前先搭建自己的思维脚手架。就像出发冒险前先选好要带的工具。推理技能树刚刚多了一个元分支。

## 4. [Chain-of-Thought Reasoning Without Prompting](https://huggingface.co/papers/2402.10200)

**`[MUTATION]`** | **ArXiv**: [2402.10200](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.10200) | **Org**: Google DeepMind | **Upvotes**: 109 | **Date**: 2024-02-16

**Skill: "Hidden Thoughts" / 技能名：「暗流涌动」**

**Category**: `Reasoning` | **Tags**: `chain-of-thought, emergent-reasoning, decoding, no-prompting`

**Source**: [papers/2024/02/2024-02-16.md, Line 9](papers/2024/02/2024-02-16.md#L9)

**EN**: MUTATION: Hidden Thoughts. You don't need to say "let's think step by step." The chain-of-thought reasoning paths already exist inside the model. By altering the decoding process to explore top-k tokens instead of greedy decoding, CoT paths naturally emerge. The model was already thinking; you just weren't looking at the right outputs. A passive skill that was always there, now activated.

**CN**: 变异：暗流涌动。你不需要说「让我们一步步思考」。思维链推理路径早已存在于模型内部。通过改变解码过程、探索 top-k token 而非贪心解码，CoT 路径自然浮现。模型一直在思考，只是你没看对输出。一个始终存在的被动技能，如今被激活了。
