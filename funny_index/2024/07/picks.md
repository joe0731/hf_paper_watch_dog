## 1. [Vision language models are blind](https://huggingface.co/papers/2407.06581)

**`[BUG REPORT]`** | **ArXiv**: [2407.06581](https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.06581) | **Org**: - | **Upvotes**: 84 | **Date**: 2024-07-10

**Skill: "Blind Spot" / 技能名：「视而不见」**

**Category**: `Multimodal` | **Tags**: `VLM-limitation`, `benchmark-failure`, `simple-visual-tasks`, `GPT-4o`, `Gemini`

**Source**: [papers/2024/07/2024-07-10.md, Line 9](papers/2024/07/2024-07-10.md#L9)

**EN**: BUG REPORT: Blind Spot. GPT-4o and Gemini 1.5 Pro fail on seven visual tasks absurdly easy for humans — counting overlapping shapes, identifying which circle is larger, reading text on rotated images. VLMs that score high on benchmarks can't do what a five-year-old can. The emperor of multimodal AI has no clothes. Critical vulnerability discovered in the vision branch.

**CN**: BUG 报告：视而不见。GPT-4o 和 Gemini 1.5 Pro 在七个人类轻松完成的视觉任务上翻车——数重叠图形、判断哪个圆更大、读旋转图片上的文字。benchmark 高分 VLMs 做不到五岁小孩能做的事。多模态 AI 的皇帝没穿衣服。视觉分支发现关键漏洞。

## 3. [SpreadsheetLLM: Encoding Spreadsheets for Large Language Models](https://huggingface.co/papers/2407.09025)

**`[COMBO]`** | **ArXiv**: [2407.09025](https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.09025) | **Org**: Microsoft Research | **Upvotes**: 139 | **Date**: 2024-07-15

**Skill: "Excel Decoder" / 技能名：「表格解码器」**

**Category**: `Multimodal` | **Tags**: `spreadsheet`, `document-understanding`, `SheetCompressor`, `enterprise`, `token-reduction`

**Source**: [papers/2024/07/2024-07-15.md, Line 9](papers/2024/07/2024-07-15.md#L9)

**EN**: COMBO DISCOVERED: Excel Decoder. Spreadsheets are the dark matter of enterprise data — massive, messy, and nearly impossible for LLMs to understand. SpreadsheetLLM introduces SheetCompressor that encodes structure, format, and layout into a format LLMs can reason about. 25x token reduction. Your AI can finally read Excel. The document-understanding and tabular-data branches just fused for a combo bonus.

**CN**: 发现组合技：表格解码器。电子表格是企业数据的暗物质——体量大、结构乱、LLM 几乎读不懂。SpreadsheetLLM 推出 SheetCompressor，把结构、格式、布局编码成 LLM 能推理的格式。25 倍 token 压缩。你的 AI 终于能读 Excel 了。文档理解与表格数据分支刚刚融合，触发连招加成。
