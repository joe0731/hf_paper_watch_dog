## 2. [Retentive Network: A Successor to Transformer for Large Language Models](https://huggingface.co/papers/2307.08621)

**`[NEW SKILL]`** | **ArXiv**: [2307.08621](https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.08621) | **Org**: Microsoft Research | **Upvotes**: 173 | **Date**: 2023-07-18

**Skill: "Triple Threat" / 技能名：「三角破局」**

**Category**: `Architecture` | **Tags**: `retention, transformer-successor, sequence-modeling, recurrence-attention`

**Source**: [papers/2023/07/2023-07-18.md, Line 9](papers/2023/07/2023-07-18.md#L9)

**EN**: NEW SKILL UNLOCKED: Triple Threat. Sequence modeling had an "impossible triangle": training parallelism, cheap inference, and good performance — you could only pick two. **CN**: 新技能解锁：三角破局。序列建模有个"不可能三角"：训练并行、低成本推理、强性能——你只能选两个。RetNet 用 retention 机制宣称三者兼得，把 recurrence 和 attention 连通。Transformer 的接班人有了竞争者。架构树可能多了一根新枝。
