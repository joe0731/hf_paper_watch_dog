## 2. [Self-RAG: Learning to Retrieve, Generate, and Critique through Self-Reflection](https://huggingface.co/papers/2310.11511)

**`[NEW SKILL]`** | **ArXiv**: [2310.11511](https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.11511) | **Org**: - | **Upvotes**: 78 | **Date**: 2023-10-19

**Skill: "Self-Check" / 技能名：「自省术」**

**Category**: `Reasoning` | **Tags**: `self-rag, retrieval-decision, reflection-tokens, self-critique`

**Source**: [papers/2023/10/2023-10-19.md, Line 10](papers/2023/10/2023-10-19.md#L10)

**EN**: NEW SKILL UNLOCKED: Self-Check. Unlike standard RAG that always retrieves, Self-RAG teaches the model to decide WHEN to retrieve, WHAT to retrieve, and then CRITIQUE its own output using special reflection tokens. The model becomes its own fact-checker. Your AI just learned quality control. The reasoning tree got a new passive: "verify before you speak."

**CN**: 新技能解锁：自省术。标准 RAG 总是检索，Self-RAG 教模型决定何时检索、检索什么，然后用特殊反思 token 批判自己的输出。模型成了自己的事实核查员。你的 AI 刚学会品控。推理树新增被动技："开口前先验货"。
