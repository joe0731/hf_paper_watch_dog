## 2. [Exponentially Faster Language Modelling](https://huggingface.co/papers/2311.10770)

**`[MUTATION]`** | **ArXiv**: [2311.10770](https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.10770) | **Org**: - | **Upvotes**: 119 | **Date**: 2023-11-21

**Skill: "Neural Narcolepsy" / 技能名：「神经嗜睡」**

**Category**: `Efficiency` | **Tags**: `conditional-computation, sparse-activation, fastbert, inference-efficiency`

**Source**: [papers/2023/11/2023-11-21.md, Line 9](papers/2023/11/2023-11-21.md#L9)

**EN**: MUTATION: Neural Narcolepsy. FastBERT uses only 0.3% of its neurons during inference (12 out of 4095) while matching full BERT performance. Conditional computation with a fast feedforward network that activates exponentially fewer neurons. Your model is 99.7% asleep and still gets the job done. The efficiency tree just mutated: "work smarter, not harder" became literal.

**CN**: 变异：神经嗜睡。FastBERT 推理时只用 0.3% 的神经元（4095 个里开 12 个）就能追平完整 BERT。条件计算 + 快速前馈网络，激活的神经元呈指数级减少。模型 99.7% 在睡觉，活照样干完。效率树刚发生变异："聪明干活不蛮干"变成了字面意思。

## 4. [Orca 2: Teaching Small Language Models How to Reason](https://huggingface.co/papers/2311.11045)

**`[NEW SKILL]`** | **ArXiv**: [2311.11045](https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.11045) | **Org**: Microsoft Research | **Upvotes**: 77 | **Date**: 2023-11-21

**Skill: "Tactical Thinking" / 技能名：「战术思维」**

**Category**: `Reasoning` | **Tags**: `orca-2, reasoning-strategies, small-model, task-adaptive, step-by-step`

**Source**: [papers/2023/11/2023-11-21.md, Line 37](papers/2023/11/2023-11-21.md#L37)

**EN**: NEW SKILL UNLOCKED: Tactical Thinking. Orca 2 teaches small models (7B–13B) to use different reasoning strategies for different problems — step-by-step for math, direct for recall, extract-then-answer for reading. Instead of one-size-fits-all thinking, the small model learns WHEN to think hard and when to think fast. The reasoning tree just got a "choose your stance" mechanic.

**CN**: 新技能解锁：战术思维。Orca 2 教小模型（7B–13B）对不同问题用不同推理策略——数学一步步来、记忆直接答、阅读先提取再答。不再一刀切，小模型学会了何时深想、何时快答。推理树新增"选架势"机制。
