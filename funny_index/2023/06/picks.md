## 3. [Extending Context Window of Large Language Models via Positional Interpolation](https://huggingface.co/papers/2306.15595)

**`[NEW SKILL]`** | **ArXiv**: [2306.15595](https://arxiv.org/abs/2306.15595) | **Org**: Meta AI | **Upvotes**: 54 | **Date**: 2023-06-28

**Skill: "Stretch" / 技能名：「弹性记忆」**

**Category**: `Architecture` | **Tags**: `position-interpolation, context-extension, long-context, llama, minimal-finetuning`

**Source**: [papers/2023/06/2023-06-28.md, Line 10](papers/2023/06/2023-06-28.md#L10)

**EN**: NEW SKILL UNLOCKED: Stretch. Position Interpolation (PI) extends LLaMA's context to 32K tokens with under 1000 fine-tuning steps. Instead of extrapolating into unknown positions, it interpolates them into the existing range. Simple, cheap, effective. The memory bar just got a stretch upgrade.

**CN**: 新技能解锁：弹性记忆。位置插值 (PI) 用不到一千步微调就把 LLaMA 的上下文拉到 32K。不往外推未知位置，而是把新位置插进已有区间。简单、省资源、好用。记忆条喜提弹性扩容。

## 4. [Orca: Progressive Learning from Complex Explanation Traces of GPT-4](https://huggingface.co/papers/2306.02707)

**`[NEW SKILL]`** | **ArXiv**: [2306.02707](https://arxiv.org/abs/2306.02707) | **Org**: Microsoft Research | **Upvotes**: 51 | **Date**: 2023-06-05

**Skill: "Copycat's Upgrade" / 技能名：「学徒进化」**

**Category**: `Training` | **Tags**: `orca, explanation-traces, progressive-learning, GPT-4-imitation, reasoning-transfer`

**Source**: [papers/2023/06/2023-06-05.md, Line 9](papers/2023/06/2023-06-05.md#L9)

**EN**: NEW SKILL UNLOCKED: Copycat's Upgrade. Orca doesn't just copy GPT-4's final answers — it learns from the full explanation traces: step-by-step reasoning, not just the conclusion. A 13B model that beats ChatGPT on some benchmarks by learning HOW to think, not just WHAT to answer. The apprentice tree just unlocked the "learn the master's process" branch.

**CN**: 新技能解锁：学徒进化。Orca 不只抄 GPT-4 的最终答案——它从完整解释轨迹里学：一步步的推理过程，而不只是结论。13B 模型在部分 benchmark 上超越 ChatGPT，因为学会了怎么想，而不只是答什么。学徒系技能树刚解锁"学师父的套路"分支。
