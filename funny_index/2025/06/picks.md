## 2. [The Illusion of Thinking: Understanding the Strengths and Limitations of
  Reasoning Models via the Lens of Problem Complexity](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.06941)

**`[BUG REPORT]`** | **ArXiv**: [2506.06941](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.06941) | **Org**:  | **Upvotes**: 16 | **Date**: 2025-06-10

**Skill: "Illusion of Thinking" / 技能名：「思维幻觉」**

**Category**: `Reasoning` | **Tags**: `illusion, pattern-matching, complexity-threshold, collapse`

**Source**: papers/2025/06/2025-06-10.md

**EN**: BUG REPORT: Illusion of Thinking. Reasoning models appear to think step by step. This analysis reveals they’re faking it: as complexity passes a threshold, performance collapses abruptly. The model isn’t reasoning; it’s pattern-matching reasoning-shaped text. The thinking is cosmetic.

**CN**: BUG 报告：思维幻觉。推理模型看起来在一步步思考。这项分析揭示它们在装：当复杂度超过某个阈值时，性能突然崩溃。模型不是在推理；是在模式匹配推理形状的文本。思考是装饰性的。
