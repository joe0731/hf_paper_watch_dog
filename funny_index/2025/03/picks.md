## 2. [LLM as a Broken Telephone: Iterative Generation Distorts Information](https://huggingface.co/papers/2502.20258)

**`[BUG REPORT]`** | **ArXiv**: [2502.20258](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.20258) | **Org**:  | **Upvotes**: 27 | **Date**: 2025-03-07

**Skill: "Broken Telephone" / 技能名：「传话游戏」**

**Category**: `Society` | **Tags**: `information-degradation, iterative-rewriting, data-quality, model-collapse`

**Source**: [papers/2025/03/2025-03-07.md, Line 201](papers/2025/03/2025-03-07.md#L201)

**EN**: BUG REPORT: Broken Telephone. When LLMs rewrite each other’s text iteratively, information degrades fast — like the children’s game of telephone. Each pass loses nuance and amplifies biases. In an era where AI trains on AI-generated text, this is the digital equivalent of inbreeding.

**CN**: BUG 报告：传话游戏。当 LLM 迭代改写彼此的文本时，信息快速退化——就像儿童传话游戏。每一轮都丢失细微差别、放大偏见。在 AI 越来越多用 AI 生成的文本来训练的时代，这就是数字版的近亲繁殖。
