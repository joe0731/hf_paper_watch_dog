**Skill: "Leaderboard Mirage" / 技能名：「排行榜海市蒃楼」**

**Category**: `Benchmarks` | **Tags**: `leaderboard, contamination, overfitting, metric-gaming, evaluation-validity`

**Source**: [papers/2025/04/2025-04-30.md, Line 52](papers/2025/04/2025-04-30.md#L52)

**EN**: NERF ALERT: Leaderboard Mirage. Leaderboard rankings are largely artifacts of test contamination, overfitting, and metric gaming — not genuine capability. The scoreboard everyone’s been chasing? It’s a mirage. Your model didn’t get smarter; it just memorized the answers.

**CN**: 削弱警告：排行榜海市蒃楼。排行榜排名在很大程度上是测试污染、过拟合和指标博弈的产物——而非真正的能力。所有人追逐的记分牌？是海市蒃楼。你的模型没变聪明；它只是记住了答案。
