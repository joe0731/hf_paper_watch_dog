## 2. [LLM-Microscope: Uncovering the Hidden Role of Punctuation in Context
  Memory of Transformers](https://huggingface.co/papers/2502.15007)

**Skill: "The Power of Punctuation" / 技能名：「标点之力」**

**Category**: `Interpretability` | **Tags**: `punctuation, memory-anchors, hidden-mechanism, context-passing`

**Source**: papers/2025/02/2025-02-24.md

**EN**: MUTATION DETECTED: The Power of Punctuation. Researchers discovered that periods and commas secretly serve as memory anchors in transformers — they store and pass critical context information across the entire sequence. Remove the punctuation, and the model’s context memory collapses. The humblest characters in the text are doing the heaviest lifting.

**CN**: 检测到突变：标点之力。研究人员发现句号和逗号在 transformer 中偷偷充当记忆锚点——它们存储并传递关键的上下文信息穿越整个序列。去掉标点，模型的上下文记忆就崩溃了。文本中最不起眼的字符在扛最重的活。
